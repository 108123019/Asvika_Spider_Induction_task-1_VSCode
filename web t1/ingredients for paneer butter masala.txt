Ingredients:
For the Paneer:

250 grams paneer, cut into cubes
2 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder (for color, adjust to taste)
Salt to taste
For the Gravy:

2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon oil
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 large onion, finely chopped
2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
2 large tomatoes, pureed
10-12 cashew nuts, soaked in warm water for 15 minutes and then ground to a smooth paste (optional, for creaminess)
1 teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder (adjust to taste)
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 teaspoon coriander powder
1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
Salt to taste
1/2 cup cream or cashew cream (made from blending soaked cashews with water until smooth)
1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander leaves (cilantro), for garnish
